Visual Studies Resources - Updated March 6, 2018

Get Involved!

  • Art Museum - http://artmuseum.utoronto.ca/

    One of the largest gallery spaces for visual art exhibitions and programming in Toronto with the director of the museum being a faculty member.

    • Works by the five Sobey Awards finalists was displayed in the museum this fall.
    • where the MVS Studio Program Graduating Exhibition and the Shelley Peterson Student Art Exhibition takes place.
    • Hosts curated gallery tours.

  • Hart House Art Committee - https://www.facebook.com/harthouseartcommittee/

    • Holds life drawings workshops, curated art gallery tours
    • HaS 4 subcommittees: acquisitions committee, arbor room subcommittee, education and outreach subcommittee and the student projects subcommittee
      • by joining the acquisitions subcommittee you can take part in the acquisition of new art for Hart House!
      • Find out more @ http://harthouse.ca/art-committee/

  • History of Art Students’ Association - https://www.facebook.com/hasa.utoronto/

    • hold workshops and talks with scholars and curators working in Toronto and the GTA
    • call for submissions if you’re interested in writing/curating

  • Visual Studies Student Projects - https://www.facebook.com/VSSP.UofT/

    • A student-run initiative to select, display, discuss, and reward exceptional student work.
    • VSSP requests work for public display in planned shows
    • Share events happening around campus and in Toronto

    The University of Toronto Shelley Peterson Student Art Exhibition showcases the artistic excellence of undergraduate students in the University of Toronto’s tri-campus visual studies programs. Selected for artistic merit and originality, these works highlight the artists’ diverse and experimental practices.
